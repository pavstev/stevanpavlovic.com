## Key Properties

- **backdrop-blur**: Creates the frosted effect
- **bg-white/10**: Semi-transparent background
- **border-white/20**: Subtle border highlights
- **shadow-2xl**: Adds depth and elevation

## Usage Guidelines

1. Use glassmorphism on elements over colorful or complex backgrounds
2. Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility
3. Combine with subtle shadows for depth
4. Keep blur intensity moderate (blur-xl to blur-2xl)
