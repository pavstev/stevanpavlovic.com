## Animation Principles

1. **Keep it subtle** - Micro-animations should enhance, not distract
2. **Use appropriate timing** - 200-500ms for most interactions
3. **Follow natural motion** - Ease-in-out feels more organic
4. **Provide feedback** - Confirm user actions visually
5. **Respect preferences** - Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`
