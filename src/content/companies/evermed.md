Evermed is a "Netflix for Doctors" providing specialized video streaming solutions for healthcare professionals. The platform delivers medical education content to 15K+ active doctors daily, processing 500+ TB of medical video content annually.

## Company Overview

Evermed is a healthcare technology startup founded in 2018, specializing in video streaming solutions for the medical and life sciences industries. The company has positioned itself as a leader in healthcare video streaming, providing a comprehensive platform for medical education, training, and content delivery.

### Core Mission

To revolutionize medical education and professional development through innovative video streaming technology, making high-quality medical content accessible to healthcare professionals worldwide.

## Technology Stack

### Backend Architecture

- **Serverless Computing**: AWS Lambda for scalable, cost-effective backend services
- **PHP/Symfony**: Robust backend framework for business logic and API development
- **Database Systems**: PostgreSQL for structured data, Redis for caching and session management
- **Cloud Storage**: AWS S3 for video storage and content delivery
- **CDN**: CloudFront for global content delivery and low-latency streaming

### Video Processing

- **AWS Elemental MediaConvert**: Serverless video transcoding and processing
- **Adaptive Bitrate Streaming**: Dynamic video quality adjustment based on network conditions
- **Video Analytics**: Real-time video performance and user engagement analytics
- **Content Management**: Advanced content management system for medical videos

### Frontend Technologies

- **React**: Modern JavaScript library for building user interfaces
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe JavaScript for better code quality
- **Node.js**: JavaScript runtime for server-side applications
- **Containerization**: Docker and Kubernetes for consistent deployment
