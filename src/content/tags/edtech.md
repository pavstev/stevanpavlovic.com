EdTech (Educational Technology) encompasses technologies designed to enhance teaching and learning processes. This includes learning management systems, educational software, online learning platforms, and digital tools for education.

## Related Concepts

- **E-Learning**: Electronic learning and online education
- **Learning Management Systems**: Platforms for course delivery
- **Educational Software**: Tools for teaching and learning
- **Digital Content**: Educational materials in digital format
- **Assessment Tools**: Evaluation and testing systems

## Use Cases

- Online course delivery and management
- Student information systems
- Educational content creation and distribution
- Learning analytics and assessment
- Virtual classrooms and collaboration tools

## Technologies

- **LMS Platforms**: Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard, Schoology
- **Content Creation**: Adobe Captivate, Articulate Storyline
- **Video Conferencing**: Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet
- **Assessment Tools**: Kahoot, Quizlet, Google Forms
- **Analytics**: Learning analytics platforms

## Best Practices

- Focus on user experience and accessibility
- Ensure data privacy and security compliance
- Provide proper training and support
- Integrate with existing educational systems
- Consider scalability and performance requirements

## Common Pitfalls

- Poor user experience leading to low adoption
- Data privacy and security issues
- Integration challenges with existing systems
- Not considering accessibility requirements
- Lack of proper support and training
